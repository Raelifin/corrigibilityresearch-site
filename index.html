Corrigibility Research Fund A program of Lightcone Infrastructure

Funding research on AI systems that help us get everything right the first time notice and correct our mistakes.

The Corrigibility Research Fund awards grants and prizes for research that advances our understanding of AI corrigibility — from pure theory to empirical work on real systems. The fund will award at least $200,000 in 2026, with the goal of impacting the AIs that actually get built.

Corrigibility, n. The property of an AI system that keeps its human principals informed and in control — one that helps us notice and fix our mistakes, including mistakes we made in building it. A corrigible AI converts a single catastrophic failure mode into an iterative process with room for error.
